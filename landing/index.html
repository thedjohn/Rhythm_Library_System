A smarter approach to drum pattern application

The Rhythm Library System is a simplification and expansion of the idea of using solo pages from the Syncopation book for sequencing rudimental or drumset patterns. The concept: using the solo pages from the middle of the Syncopation book for sequencing a select group of rudimental patterns or drumset patterns.

The result is a system that is accessible to beginners, challenging for advanced players, and immediately useful for teachers looking for a structured approach to building student vocabulary.
