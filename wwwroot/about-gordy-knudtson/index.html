Gordy Knudtson

Drummer, Educator, and Creator of the Rhythm Library System

Gordy Knudtson has been the drummer for the Steve Miller Band for over 30 years, appeared on landmark recordings, performed on world tours, and built one of the most respected careers in professional drumming. He is also a dedicated educator and the creator of the Rhythm Library System.

Career

Gordy Knudtson joined the Steve Miller Band in the early 1990s and has remained the band's drummer ever since. As a member of the Steve Miller Band, Gordy was inducted into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame. He has toured extensively across North America and internationally, performing for audiences of tens of thousands.

The Rhythm Library System

Drawing on decades of experience as both a performer and educator, Gordy developed the Rhythm Library System — an 83-page PDF method that creates hundreds of sticking pattern combinations using a structured rhythmic framework. The method is designed to be accessible to beginners, challenging for advanced players, and immediately useful for teachers.

Explore the Method

  • Three Camps Drum Exercise — the classic rudimental sequence as a practice framework
  • Drum Sticking Patterns — build hand control, speed, and creative vocabulary
  • Drum Independence Exercises — develop real kit coordination
  • Learn Drum Notation — read drum sheet music with confidence
